🎙 Recording tips

Website video: Record landscape (16:9). Plain background or a clean office setup. Look directly at the camera, not the screen. Speak at a natural pace — don't rush.
Social video: Record portrait (9:16) for Reels. Bright lighting. Start talking immediately — no intro. First 2 seconds determine if someone keeps watching.
Captions: Always add captions to the social version — most people watch Reels with sound off. CapCut auto-generates them in one click.
Upload: Website video → YouTube or Vimeo (unlisted is fine) → paste the URL and Claude will embed it. Social video → upload directly to IG/FB as a Reel.
