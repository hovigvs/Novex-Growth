Step 01

Customer Reaches Out

A customer messages Novex Growth directly on WhatsApp. Maybe they saw a post, got a referral, or are an existing loyal customer who knows what they want. Layla responds instantly — day or night.

24/7 Response Instant Reply No Wait Time
Step 02

Layla Greets & Engages

Layla introduces herself warmly and immediately asks the right questions to understand the customer's needs — no menus to scroll, no website to navigate. Just a natural conversation.

English Arabic Turkish Auto-detects Language
Step 03

Understanding the Occasion

Layla asks about the occasion and guest count — the two things that determine everything. With this information she can suggest the perfect items and quantities without the customer needing to know the menu.

Smart Questioning Quantity Planning
Step 04

Personalized Suggestions

Based on the occasion and group size, Layla recommends specific dishes with prices. She also shares the Novex Growth WhatsApp Catalogue so customers can visually browse items — photos, names and prices — without leaving the chat.

WhatsApp Catalogue Smart Recommendations Live Pricing
Step 05

Food Images Sent by Layla

When a customer asks about a specific dish, Layla sends the actual food photo directly in the chat — just like a personal recommendation. The customer sees exactly what they're ordering before committing.

Real Food Photos Visual Confirmation Builds Confidence
Step 06

Order Confirmed in Chat

Layla compiles the full order — items, quantities, total, pickup date and location — and presents a clean summary for the customer to confirm. Everything documented in the chat thread both parties keep.

Full Order Summary Pickup Details 48hr Notice
Step 07

Stripe Payment Link Sent

Layla sends a secure Stripe payment link directly in WhatsApp. Customer taps, pays in their browser, returns to WhatsApp to see confirmation. Full payment upfront — no deposits, no back and forth. Novex Growth receives an instant paid order notification.
