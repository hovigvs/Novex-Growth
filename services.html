Custom AI systems built for your business

Every engagement is scoped to your actual workflow. No generic packages — just the right systems, built the right way, and managed for you ongoing.

AI chatbots

An AI agent that lives on your website and handles customer conversations around the clock — answering questions, recommending products or services, capturing lead details, and booking appointments, all without any human involvement.

  • Responds in the customer's language automatically
  • Voice and text input supported
  • Trained on your actual menu, services, and policies
  • Named and branded as your own agent

WhatsApp & SMS automation

Broadcast campaigns, reactivation sequences, and conversational ordering flows that run entirely on WhatsApp — where your customers already are. The AI handles the full conversation, from first message to confirmed booking or paid order.

  • Broadcast campaigns to your existing customer list
  • Reactivation sequences for lapsed customers
  • Full ordering and booking flows in conversation
  • Dietary, allergy, and preference capture built in

AI voice receptionists

A natural-sounding AI voice agent that answers every inbound call — even after hours, on weekends, or when your team is busy. It handles common questions, captures enquiry details, and routes urgent calls appropriately.

  • Never misses a call, any time of day
  • Natural conversation, not a phone menu
  • Captures caller details and enquiry type
  • Forwards urgent calls to a real person when needed

CRM & booking integration

Every lead and booking captured by your AI systems flows automatically into a centralised CRM — organised, tracked, and ready to action. No manual data entry, no leads falling through the cracks.

  • Full pipeline visibility across all leads
  • Automated follow-up sequences
  • Integrates with your existing booking tools
  • Reporting and order status at a glance

Outbound growth campaigns

Most businesses wait for customers to come to them. We flip that — building targeted contact lists of companies and individuals who are likely to need your services, then running automated campaigns across email and WhatsApp to bring them in proactively.

  • Targeted B2B and B2C contact list building
  • Automated email campaigns to corporations and local businesses
  • WhatsApp list growth and broadcast campaigns
  • Lapsed customer reactivation sequences
  • Promotional and seasonal campaign automation
