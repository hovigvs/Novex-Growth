About

We started because most businesses were losing customers without knowing it

Every missed call, unanswered message, or slow response is a customer who moved on to someone else. For most small and mid-sized service businesses, that's not a strategy problem — it's a capacity problem. There are only so many hours in a day, and no one can be available around the clock.

That's the gap we built Novex Growth to close.

We started after seeing firsthand how much revenue service businesses leave on the table simply because they couldn't respond fast enough. A catering company missing an enquiry at 10pm. A venue losing a booking because no one picked up on a Saturday. The business wasn't the problem — the systems were.

AI has changed what's possible. The same technology that powers enterprise-level customer service can now be built into a local catering company, a venue, a restaurant, or a salon — at a fraction of the cost, and without needing a technical team to run it. But most business owners don't know where to start, and most AI tools are built for people who already know how to use them.

That's where we come in. We build the system, integrate it into how you already work, and keep it running — so you get the results without the complexity.

What we believe

AI should work for your business, not require a second business to manage it

Every system we build is custom-scoped to your actual workflow, your customers, and your goals. We don't sell packages or templates. We build what you actually need.

The best AI automation is invisible to your customers

They shouldn't know they're talking to a system — they should just feel like your business is always on top of things, always responsive, always professional.

We're partners, not vendors

We build it, we run it, you own it. We stay involved after launch to make sure the system keeps performing — not hand you a login and disappear.
